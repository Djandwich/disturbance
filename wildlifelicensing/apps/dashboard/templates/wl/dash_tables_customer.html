Welcome to the Wildlife Licensing Online Portal

On this site you will be able to apply online for a wildlife licence, manage your current licences and submit returns.

Licences

Licences can be obtained by following the application process and receiving approval from a licensing officer. You may apply for as many licences as required. Once you've applied, the status of your applications will be shown in the Applications table below. To begin the process, click the link below.

Apply for a new wildlife licence online

Once your licence has been approved, you will be notified via email with your licence attached. A summary of all you current and past licences is shown in the Licences table below.

Returns

Depending on the type of licence you hold, you may be required to submit returns on a regular basis. These are accessible in the Returns table below. The table will contain any past returns submitted as well as the next return due.
